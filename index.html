Interesting Facts about Origami

1.Origami is derived from two Japanese words: Ori (folded) and Kami (paper). 2.Before that, the art was called “orikata” (folded shapes). 3. An ancient Japanese legend says if you fold one thousand cranes you will be granted a wish 4.Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture. 5.In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin. 6.Many studies assert that origami was invented by the Japanese about a thousand years ago, but its roots may well be in China. 7.It is also highly probable that the process of folding was applied to other materials before paper was invented, so the origins of recreational folding may lie with cloth or leather.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana species.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outsanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons also have excellent hearing abilities.
  4. In Old Times the Pigeons were used as postman or you can call post animals like Owls.
